Kale Salad

Description

It is so easy to make and healthy. It is delicious; my family loves it. I take it to summer picnics and everyone raves.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head kale, stemmed, leaves cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 lemon juiced
  • 1/4 cup silvered raw almonds
  • sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. place all ingredients in bowl and mix
